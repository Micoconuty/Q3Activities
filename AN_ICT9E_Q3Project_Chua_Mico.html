Fire Safety Awareness Month

When a fire starts in your home, you may only have two minutes to escape. Having a smoke alarm and a fire escape plan can easily save lives.

Fire Safety Tips at Home

Make sure to know the number of your local fire station | Philippines - 160 / 911.
Keep matches out of children's reach.
Install smoke on every level of your home, even inside bedrooms.
Make sure to test smoke alarms every month.
Create a fire escape plan and practice it twice a year
If a fire were to occur in your home, Get Out, Stay Out, and Call for Help. NEVER go back inside
